Guest services

Laundry & vehicle services

Little conveniences that make a stay easy — just ask at the front desk and we'll arrange it, charged straight to your room.

Laundry service

Same-day washing, drying and pressing with per-item pricing. Drop your laundry at the front desk before 9am and it's back the same evening.

Airport & town shuttle

Scheduled pick-ups and drop-offs for Girua Airport and Popondetta town. Book your shuttle at check-in or the day before travel.

Charters & day trips

Need a vehicle for meetings, site visits, or a run out to the province? Talk to the front desk about charter transport arrangements.
